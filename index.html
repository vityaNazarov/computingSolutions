San Francisco Bay Computing Solutions is a network of IT professionals located in San Francisco Bay Area to provide a full scale of IT services and to support our client markets and their Information Technology service needs. We are organized into primary practice areas, such as remote and on-site IT Services Outsourcing, IT Projects implementation and IT Support for medium size and small businesses of the San Francisco Bay Area.

Your Complete Technology Provider

At Computing Solutions, we keep IT simple, reliable and affordable. We are helping businesses in Northern California succeed by filling the IT gap through our proactive model and customized solutions.

What makes us different from other IT Services companies is our approach

  • Your investment in technology must be a stepping stone into future systems;
  • Our goal is to create a long term Business Partnership with you;
  • The objective is proactive, scalable IT systems, implemented to industry best practice;
  • We take the time to understand your business , how you and your staff work, in and out of the work place, how you operate now and would like to operate in the future with and without technology;
  • We work together to determine the 'right' systems for your business;
  • We aim to take on the management of all your Business IT Systems , including monitoring your expenses and budgets to save your money;
  • We work with you on your business IT strategy, through discussions with your key staff, formalise a plan to save your time and money;
  • We take on the 'BIG PICTURE' thinking for the technology in your business with 12 and/or 36 month visions for technology that align with your business plan;
  • An agent is installed to provide instant remote support saving time and money If you currently have preferred suppliers or contracts in place.

Computing Solutions will help you get the most out of these providers, ensuring your hard earned dollars are getting what you paid for. We will take on the role of IT Manager for you, representing your business to the vendor, advocating your desired outcomes and leaving you more time to get your job done. Get more from your Business IT Systems with our approach. Your business can leverage the knowledge and technology available to larger enterprises through your partnership with Computing Solutions. Where possible and proven it is the best solution for your business we are offering cloud computing services for the next wave in technology.
